The American Society of Civil Engineers is a international association dedicated to the professional, academic, and personal growth of Civil Engineers. UCLA's student chapter is the best way to make the most of your Civil experience, with Nationally-winning projects, close industry and faculty ties, and social events throughout the quarter. Our projects, including Concrete Canoe, Seismic Design, Geotechnical Design, Steel Bridge, Environmental Design, Surveying, and Concrete Sports, allow students of all years and skill levels to practice the material presented in the classroom. To learn more about ASCE and everything we have to offer, please visit ASCE Open House on Wednesday of Second Week, more information will be available on our website during Fall. Other professional and industry events include the ASCE Fall Career Fair, Graduate Panel, Internship Panel, ASCE Winter Career Fair, and Infosessions. Other events include our annual Big Bear Ski Trip, Regional Conferences, National Conferences, Socials, and Student-Professor BBQs. More information will be available on our website and Facebook page.
