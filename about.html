About student organizations

How joining clubs and projects will enrich your college experience at UCLA.

What is a student organization?

One of the integral parts to life within UCLA and UCLA Engineering is participation in student groups/organizations. There are over 30 student groups, and several hundred students help serve and lead these groups. Each student group has its own demographics and purposes. However, all of them combine to provide the best possible experience for every student here at UCLA.

Student organizations present a unique opportunity for students because they are entirely student run with only advisory support from faculty. As a result, many of the activities hosted by student organizations are created from the standpoint of a student rather than the school.

They range in size and purpose: from a few individuals to several hundred, from community service engineering groups to honor societies. No matter your interest or personality, there is a student organization that would love to have you!

Why join a student organization?

With a lot of coursework and plenty of other activities available on campus, participating in student organizations may appear to be the last thing on your mind. However, participating in student organizations provide many benefits at the same time. They include:

  • Diversified experience to put on your resume
  • Creation of a network of talented individuals
  • Opportunities to meet with companies
  • Change to improve UCLA Engineering

Throughout the entire nation, students are earning engineering degrees from several great universities. With their education, they are qualified to solve almost any technical issue thrown at them. As a result, many companies are beginning to look at the non-technical skill sets of their candidates, which aren't usually taught in a classroom. That is where participation in student organizations can be highly beneficial. They provide the chance to develop skills such as public speaking, management, and teamwork that are just as necessary to succeed in the real world. Furthermore, companies understand the talent found in student organization leadership and go to them first for talent. So find one that fits your desires and try it out!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a club?

Each club has their own methods of membership. Some require dues, others do not. For more details, visit their profile by clicking their name on this list of groups.

How do I join a club?

Each club and each position within each club has their own time commitment. Before committing to a leadership position within any club, ask current officers within that club about the time commitment for the position that you are interested in.
